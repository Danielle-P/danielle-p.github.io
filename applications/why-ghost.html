Why I'm Applying to Ghost

Over the last 10 years I’ve had the benefit of working across a wide range of organisations, from large corporations to early stage start-ups and I’ve learned a lot from each experience. One pattern I’ve noticed is how frequent pivots in direction resulting from investor pressure or a focus on aggressive growth can in reality lead to a lack of clarity, teams losing sight of the people they’re building for, and ultimately a loss of trust and user loyalty. As a designer, I do my best work in environments where users, not investors, are at the centre of decision-making and teams are connected to the people using their product.

I am looking for a company that doesn’t chase trends or follow investor pressures, but instead has a clear understanding of who they are creating the product for, and confidently sticks to that mission. Ghost stands out to me because of its non-profit, open-sourced model, the transparency with which the company operates, and the clarity around its purpose and target users.

In short, I am hoping to find a company whose values and way of working align with my own; that is, working intentionally and thoughtfully to solve problems well for those who are putting their trust into the product and company.
