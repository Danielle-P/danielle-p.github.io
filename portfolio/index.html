Portfolio

In my role as Senior UX Designer at KPMG, I work on designing and building bespoke Customer Experience Dashboards which are used internally by our clients to analyse customer experience and satisfaction data taken from surveys. I have worked most recently with household-name brands in the financial, airline and energy sectors to deliver a variety of solutions to help those businesses measure performance through customer experience data.

Outside of work I am part of the two-person team that created the app MealsUp, which is available on the App Store. MealsUp is designed to be a simple, easy to use meal scheduling app with additional features including a groceries list automatically generated upon scheduling a meal and a method feature which allows users to follow a specific recipe whilst cooking.
