Dashboard Design

In my current role at KPMG I design fully responsive enterprise dashboard solutions, with a focus on data visualisations and customer analytics. My role also includes building the front-ends for these dashboards.

Dashboard project for a client in the Airline industry

I designed and built a dashboard site to be used internally by a well-known airline company. The purpose of the site was to allow managers and the insight team to monitor their customer satisfaction scores against their targets, analyse customer experience data and prioritise actions accordingly.

The site was intended for several different user groups, including staff managing ground operations teams, cabin crew managers and Head Office staff reporting to the leadership team. Around half of these users would be accessing the site only on mobile devices as they were not office-based. I tailored pages to each user group, with simple score-based views for the on-the-ground users who needed to be able to quickly identify their performance against their targets and see how their individual team members were performing. The Head Office and management level views were more complex, showing more detailed analytics and including the functionality for those users to see a high level overview with the option to drill down further into the data (for example by filtering the results) and to create/save reports.

I created mockups and wireframes for both mobile and desktop, and sought feedback from the relevant stakeholders on both sets before creating the final prototypes using Sketch and InVision. I followed Agile best practices when building the front-end of the site.

Dashboard project for a client in the Banking sector

I designed a mobile-friendly site to be used by a banking client to monitor its performance against its KPIs in relation to customer satisfaction and the customer journey. A key part of the design was a league table style regional rankings page, showing the top performing regions by customer satisfaction score, with the option to view details of best practices followed in those regions to drive improvement across the whole business. I designed this site using Photoshop and used InVision for the clickable prototype.
