Dashboard Design

In my current role at KPMG I design fully responsive enterprise dashboard solutions, with a focus on data visualisations and customer analytics. My role also includes building the front-ends for these dashboards.

The purpose of the dashboards is to provide management and insight professionals with customer insights to help them to make key business decisions. The dashboard sites I have designed include tailored pages for different user groups across the business.

Due to client confidentiality I am unfortunately unable to share the specific designs I have created during my time at KPMG.

However, to illustrate the type of projects I have enjoyed working on, I have recreated some mock-ups of dashboards and data visualisations which are based on those I have designed and built. I have also included the user flows, site maps and initial wireframes.
