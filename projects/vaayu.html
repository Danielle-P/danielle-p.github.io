Carbon Emissions Tracking

I was the sole product designer at Vaayu, a climate-tech startup helping companies calculate, track, and reduce their carbon emissions.

The carbon emissions platform centred around 3 core elements:

  • a dashboard to track and visualise emissions over time
  • a scenario modelling and target-setting tool
  • a feature to allow customers to track the accuracy and completeness of data across their supply chain.

I led the end-to-end design across all three areas, collaborating with climate specialists and industry experts to distil complex data and workflows into a clear, user-friendly product.

Full details of the projects I worked on at Vaayu are confidential, but for more information about my role here more generally contact me.
