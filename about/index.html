About

Hi, I'm Danielle. My background is originally in law and, after 5 years of working in banking law, I made the switch to UX design through a graduate scheme and have been working as a product/UX designer for the last 7 years.

My design approach focuses on creating intuitive and elegant experiences that solve real problems and deliver customer value. I am able to apply the problem-solving and analytical skills I developed in my legal training to develop a deep understanding of users and deliver value to customers. I have worked on a variety of digital products, including B2B dashboards, SaaS platforms and iOS apps. I also have experience in front-end coding - this informs my design process and helps me to work effectively with engineers. I enjoy working with engineers early on in my design process as I believe working as a cross-functional team, and the diversity of thought that brings, is the best way to solve users' problems.

Outside of work I have completed a number of projects including co-founding and designing the MealsUp iOS App, a meal-scheduling App which has over 15,000 users globally and a rating of 4.7 stars on the App Store. I was solely responsible for the visual and UX design of MealsUp.

Aside from design I love running, hiking in the mountains and travelling.
