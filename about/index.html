About Me

I am a Senior UX Designer at KPMG, working closely with a small team to design and implement bespoke client dashboards visualising customer experience and satisfaction data. The dashboards I design and create are based on a product developed over a number of years, but the design and front-end build of the produc is bespoke to each of our clients. My role is twofold - to lead on the design of the client implementation of the dashboard, including requirements gathering, meeting with clients at scoping sessions and creating the wireframe; 2) building the front end of the site, which also includes writing the code for data visualisations in razor.

I have worked with household name clients in the Energy, Banking and Airline sectors

Outside of work I am part of the two-person team that created the app MealsUp, which is available on the App Store. MealsUp is designed to be a simple, easy to use meal scheduling app with additional features including a groceries list automatically generated upon scheduling a meal and a method feature which allows users to follow a specific recipe whilst cooking.
