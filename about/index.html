About

Hi, I'm Danielle, a product designer with a background in law. After my degree, I worked in banking law before making a career change and re-training as a UX Designer via a graduate scheme. I’ve since gained 7 years experience in Product Design and have worked on a variety of platforms in that time, including an iOS app which I co-created, alongside gaining some experience in front-end development.

My design approach focuses on creating intuitive and elegant experiences that solve real problems and pains for users. I am able to apply the problem-solving and analytical skills I developed in my legal training to develop a deep understanding of users and deliver value to customers. I love working on designs that remove friction and make people’s day-to-day lives easier. I am happiest when I am able to also spend some time on the small details, which I believe can help transform a good experience into a delightful one.

I enjoy working closely with PMs, engineers and data teams to collaborate on my designs as I believe the diversity of thought that comes from working as a cross-functional team is the best way to solve users' problems.

Outside of work I have completed a number of projects including co-founding and designing the MealsUp iOS App, a meal-scheduling App which has over 15,000 users globally and a rating of 4.7 stars on the App Store. I was solely responsible for the visual and UX design of MealsUp.

In my spare time I love running, hiking in the mountains and travelling. I’m currently based in Barcelona.
