About Me

In my role as Senior UX Designer at KPMG, I work within the Customer Experience Cloud (CX Cloud) team. CX Cloud is a ‘voice of the customer’ dashboard solution designed to provide management and insight professionals with an understanding of their customer needs and pain points in order to drive organisational change and improvements in their customers’ experience. The product itself is highly customisable and I am responsible for the UX design and the front-end build of the dashboards which are designed and implemented on a client-by-client basis.

I have worked most recently with household-name brands in the energy, airline and financial sectors to deliver a variety of solutions to help those businesses use their customer experience data to measure and drive improvements in performance.

Outside of work I have completed a number of projects including co-founding and designing the MealsUp iOS App. MealsUp is designed to be a simple, easy to use meal-scheduling App with additional features such as a groceries list automatically generated upon scheduling a meal. As part of the two person team that created the App, I was solely responsible for the visual and UX design. MealsUp has over 9,000 users and currently has a rating of 4.6 stars on the App Store.
