About

I have over 4 years' experience in UX design and front-end development. In my current role as Senior UX Designer at KPMG I design and deliver ‘voice of the customer’ dashboard solutions to help businesses understand their customer needs and pain points, to leverage their customer satisfaction data to drive organisational change and improve their customers’ experience.

My design approach focuses heavily on understanding the user’s needs, business context and goals. I am able to apply the problem-solving and analytical skills I developed in my previous legal training to create designs which provide solutions to user problems and deliver value to our clients’ businesses. My experience in front-end coding also informs my design process, enabling me to create component-based designs which are feasible and can be implemented within project timelines. I enjoy working with developers to implement my designs and solve any problems that arise during the build phase.

I currently work with product and engineering teams to design the UX and UI of features, new components and modules for our core dashboard product. The product itself is highly customisable and I am responsible for the UX design and the front-end build of bespoke implementations of the product for our clients. Our client implementations include household-name brands in the energy, airline and financial sectors and have over 40,000 unique logins per month.

Outside of work I have completed a number of projects including co-founding and designing the MealsUp iOS App, a meal-scheduling App which has over 14,000 users globally and a rating of 4.7 stars on the App Store. I was solely responsible for the visual and UX design of MealsUp.
